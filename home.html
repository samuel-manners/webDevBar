Our Friendly Members Club

Here at Bordon Working Mens Club we aim to create an amazing environment for everyone! Wether you want to go for a cheeky game of Bingo or listen to live artists and DJs we have your back!

Hall Rental

We have an amazing hall available to rent for you to put your event on! Available whether your a member of our club or not, our hall is fitted with lighting to create the atmosphere you want and our friendly bar staff will be available to serve you drinks throughout your time here!

Packed with everything you need

Even when events aren't on, you will find entertainment here. Our amazing Jukebox has all the songs you could ask for and even works as a photobooth! We have slot and ticket machines for anyone who wants to have a chance at winning that extra cash, and lets not forget we have Pool Tables available for just 50p a go as well as darts. We want to make sure your experience is packed with everything you could need!
