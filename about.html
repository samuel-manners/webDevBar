About Us

Bordon Working Mens Club is a members club based in the town of Bordon, Hampshire. We run many different events and offer a variety of drinks and snacks. Also available is Hall Booking so you can have your Birthday Party, Wedding Partys and events here, even if you are a member (subject to availability).


Tones of things to do!

Jukebox

Touchtunes

Our Touchtunes Jukebox is packed with so many songs you'll never run out of choices! Also working as a photobooth get those group pictures you want of your night out!

PLAY SOME POOL

Our Pool Tables

With two amazing pool tables costing just 50p a go why not play a game of pool whilst enjoying your pint at our venue? Want to get competative, join our clubs local pool competitions and compete across the local region with other clubs and pubs!

DARTS, DARTS, DARTS

Play Darts

Do you enjoy playing darts? Why not enjoy your drink in our Lounge Bar and play a game or two of darts?

Feel more competative? Join our darts league and compete against other local venues!

SLOTS & TICKETS

Chance to slot in some money!

Want to try your luck on our machines? We have a variety of slots games and ticket machines with chances of winning big prizes!

Our Snooker Facilities!

We have our very own Snooker room! Only available to people over the age of 16 go play some snooker and relax with peace in mind!
